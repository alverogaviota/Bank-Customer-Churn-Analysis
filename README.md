Data Analysis

According to the visualizations, we can summarize a few things regarding customer churn

Spain is the only country with the least customer churn.
Females tend to leave the bank more than the male.
Most customers leaving are in their early 30s to late 50s.
Longer customers with the bank do not guarantee their stay.
Most customers who are leaving only use one product/service.
The majority of customers who own credit cards are also leaving.
Nonactive members tend to leave the bank.
The majority of customers have zero balances in their accounts.
Many customers have good credit scores.
Predictive Model

Based on the metrics results, the model does well predicting the customers who are staying (due to the imbalanced class). However, there's still a need for improvement in predicting customers who are leaving.
